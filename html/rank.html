Rank Structure

Prerequisites, Duties & Privileges

Basic Responsibilities:

  • Conduct yourself in accordance with our Rules & Regulations
  • Vote daily for the community on armaclans.com
  • Read news in Community Announcements
  • Meet minimum attendance ratio of 1 event per 2 months. If that ratio cannot be fulfilled, Leave of Absence (LoA) needs to be requested

Recruit [Rec]

Prerequisites:

  • Minimum age for membership is 21+
  • Following time zones are accepted: from UTC-2 to UTC+3
  • Members must own a legit copy of Arma 3
  • Having a working microphone and intermediate English skills are required
  • Has filled out an application on the website
  • Has completed the recruitment interview

NOTE: Rare exceptions are possible for Recruits between the age of 18-20 if their application is evaluated convincing enough.


Duties:

  • Basic Responsibilities.
  • Completes the Checklist for Recruits
  • Successfully pass Basic Qualification Form within 8 weeks of joining the community
  • Has to attend at least 5 events within 8 weeks of joining the community

Privileges:

  • Can attend events (requires completed Mod Assessment)

Reservist [Res]

Prerequisites:

  • Has successfully completed Basic Qualification Form - if Recruit
  • Is a Carpe Noctem Alumni rejoining the community - rare case
  • On events has shown esprit de corps

Duties:

  • Basic Responsibilities

Privileges:

  • Can vote in official community polls
  • Can attend monthly Staff Meeting and Q&A

Grunt [Gnt]

Prerequisites:

  • Has attended 1/3rd of events in last 2 month period. - recurring

Duties:

  • Basic Responsibilities
  • Promote community ethos

Privileges:

  • Gets to pick player slots before Reservists
  • Can vote in official community polls
  • Can attend monthly Staff Meeting and Q&A

Specialist [SPC]

Prerequisites:

  • Has the time and the enthusiasm to do organisational work
  • Is reliable and dedicated in his work
  • Is elected by SSgt

Duties:

  • Basic Responsibilities
  • Fulfills tasks and duties assigned by SSgt which may include:
    • Managing an administrative squad
    • Managing a Branch
    • Other various tasks which require special permission level

Staff Absences:

  • Inform about absence in advance so projects can be continued
  • If a staff member can not perform his duties for up to 6 weeks, another staff member will take over the duties for the time being
  • If a staff member can not perform his duties for longer than 6 weeks, a replacement will be found for him

Privileges:

  • Gets to pick player slots before Reservists & Grunts
  • Can vote in official community polls
  • Can attend monthly Staff Meeting and Q&A

Staff Sergeant [SSgt]

Prerequisites:

  • Has the time and the enthusiasm to do organisational work
  • Is reliable and dedicated in his work
  • Is in it for the long haul

Duties:

  • Basic Responsibilities
  • Attends Staff Meetings
  • Supervises, guides and helps all Staff members
  • Makes sure the esprit de corps and the ethos of the community is not compromised
  • Meet on weekly basis to share updates and develop ongoing and future projects
  • Counsels with community members about developments of the community to get their point of view and feedback

Privileges:

  • Gets to pick player slots before Reservists & Grunts
  • Can vote in official community polls
  • Can vote on final decisions concerning the communities visions and overall strategy

Contributor Tag

Prerequisites:

  • Agrees to donate $2.5.- a month for 6 months in advance, amount due: $15.- (Bronze)
  • Agrees to donate $5.- a month for 6 months in advance, amount due: $30.- (Silver)
  • Agrees to donate $10.- a month for 6 months in advance, amount due: $60.- (Gold)

If a member wishes to donate but can't afford $15.-, $30.- or $60.- in one payment, we advise to save up until he can make the single payment. This lowers administrative work for the community and eliminates potential scenarios where we have to run after promised donations on a monthly basis. Members can always donate more than $60.- but we can't offer them any additional rewards for doing so.


Privileges:

  • Unlock unique tag on TeamSpeak and the website
  • Will be named in a "thank you" post on the website
  • Access to CNTO Trello workflow

Interviewing

Prerequisites:

  • Is mature and has good people skills
  • Has good technical knowledge to resolve common problems

Duties:

  • Answers any inquiries of guest on TS
  • Arranges and conducts interviews with applicants
  • Introduce recruits to our modset
  • Helps Recruits and troubleshoots technical difficulties

Mission Making

Prerequisites:

  • Has basic understanding of making Arma 3 missions

Duties:

  • Prepares and hosts official Coop or PvP missions

Research & Development

Prerequisites:

  • Has advanced understanding of Arma 3 and underlying code

Duties:

Does at least on of the following:

  • Works on development of CNTO templates
  • Researches and tests potential modset additions
  • Ensures quality and performance of the modset

Training

Prerequisites:

  • Is mature and has good people skills
  • Has experience in game and is willing to teach others
  • Has good knowledge of the ShackTac Guide

Duties:

  • Prepares and hosts training or qualification sessions
  • Assists in training or qualification sessions

Web Design

Prerequisites:

  • Is proficient with HTML, CSS, Javascript, Python or other web oriented languages

Duties:

  • Maintains cisual clarity of the website
  • Helps extending and improving website functionality

NOTE: One can be both Web Designer and Graphic Designer or can specialize in one of the two
