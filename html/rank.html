Rank Structure

Prerequisites, Duties & Privileges

Carpe Noctem runs on a system of government by representation. If Specialists, Grunts or Recruits (also referred to as the "community player base") would like to propose changes they can relay their withses to their assigned JrNCO. JrNCO will then bring these points on behalf of them in the next monthly NCO meeting. The final decision on the direction of the community takes is in the hands of three officer ranks, CO, XO and SCNO, who have equal voting power over each other. The officers must base their foting decisions in the interest of all members and must confer with the NCOs and JrNCOs in a monthly meeting before any major decision is made definitive. JrNCOs should argue on behalf of the community player base while the officers represent the needs of management, meta gameplay & business. NCOs are meant to be the neutral tipping point on the scale.

Please note that all donations, including the rank-dependent ones, are not reimbursable. That includes reasons like long term LoAs, demotions or discharges on accound of undesirable behaviour as stated in our Rules & Regulations. Any surplus of donations can only be spent on investments towards the community: e.g. upgrading server hardware / software, elite armaclans.com membership, professional ar work & webdesign or other goodies like printing shirts and mailing them to members to celebrate anniversaries etc.

Being part of any branch helps turning the cogs that are running this community. You will have to be at least the rank of Grunt to apply for any of the branches. When applying for rank promotions we will consider your investment in branch work.

Community Overview

  • Officers:
    CO, XO, SNCO
    • Develop ideas to improve the community
    • Manage the community as a whole
    • Are head of one organisational branch
    • Share equal voting rights on final decisions
  • NCOs
    • Are head of one organisational branch
    • Do minor administration work
    • Manage and guide the JrNCO's
  • JrNCOs
    • Represent the Community Player Base in NCO meetings
    • Manage and guide the Community Player Base
  • Community Player Base:
    Specialists, Grunts, Recruits
    • Enjoy the results of branches' work
    • Can contact JrNCOs to relay their concerns or wishes

Basic responsibilities:

  • Conduct yourself in accordance with our Rules & Regulations.
  • Vote daily for the community on armaclans.com.
  • Reply to event invitations on the website.
  • Be active on the forums.
  • Help out recruits whenever you can.

Recruit [Rec]

Prerequisite:

  • Has filled out an application on the website.
  • Has completed the recruitment interview.

Duties:

  • Basic responsibilities.
  • Complete the checklist for recruits
  • Does the mandatory reading.
  • Successfully complete Grunt Qualification Form within 8 weeks of joining community.

Privileges:

  • Can attend community events after an Interviewer has confirmed that mods are working properly.

Grunt [Gnt]

Prerequisite:

  • Has successfully completed Grunt Qualification Form - normal case.
  • On events has shown esprit de corps.
  • Is a Carpe Noctem Alumni rejoining the community - rare case.

Duties:

  • Basic responsibilities.

Privileges:

  • Can pick slots within support elements. (Vehicle Crews, MMG, MAT etc.)
  • Gets to pick player slots before Recruits.
  • Can vote in community polls.

Specialist [SPC]

Prerequisite:

  • Has above-average proficiency and familiarity with the game.
  • Has above-average knowledge of how the community works on an organisational level.

Duties:

  • Basic responsibilities.
  • Is assisting one JrNCO in all his duties - especially if JrNCO is not available.
  • Assists players in-game and helps them improve their skills and tactics (point out mistakes in a constructive manner).
  • Will take over fireteam leads if asked by HQ.

Privileges:

  • Gets to pick player slots before Recruits & Grunts.
  • Can vote in community polls.
  • Will be mentored by an assigned JrNCO.

Technical Specialist [TSPC]

Prerequisite:

  • Is a member with a very specific technical skill set that benefits the community as a whole, such as coding, design, server administration etc. but who is not willing or able to lead / advance to ranks beyond SPC.

Duties:

  • Basic responsibilities.
  • Will work on technical projects.

Privileges:

  • Gets to pick player slots before Recruits & Grunts.
  • Can vote in community polls.

Junior NCO [JrNCO]

Prerequisite:

  • Has completed FTL Training Program.
  • Has the time and the enthusiasm to do off-battlefield work.
  • Is not a flavour-of-the-month.

Duties:

  • Basic responsibilities.
  • Will take over fireteam leads if asked by HQ.
  • Is online on TS as much as possible.
  • Attends NCO meetings.
  • Helps out Recruits, Grunts & Specialists with their issues.
  • Is 1IC of an administrative squad.
    • Creates statistics on squad member attendance.
    • Represents squad members issues / suggestions in monthly NCO meetings.
    • Answers questions of squad members.

Privileges:

  • Gets to pick player slots before Recruits, Grunts & Specialists.
  • Can vote in community polls.
  • Can actively shape the community through their work.
  • Will be mentored by an assigned NCO.

Non Commissioned Officer [NCO]

  • Has completed SL Training Program.
  • Has the time and the enthusiasm to do off-battlefield work.
  • Is in it for the long haul.

Duties:

  • Will take over squad leads if asked by HQ.
  • Is online on TS as much as possible.
  • Attends NCO meetings.
  • Helps out Recruits, Grunts, Specialists and JrNCOs with their issues.

Contributor Tag

Prerequisite:

  • Has to be minimum rank of Grunt.
  • Agrees to donate $5.- a month for 6 months in advance, ammount due: $30.- (Silver).
  • Agrees to donate $10.- a month for 6 months in advance, ammount due: $60.- (Gold).

If a member wishes to donate but can't affort $30.- or $60.- in one payment, we advise to save up until he can make the single payment. The reason for this is to lessen administrative work for the community and to eliminate potential scenarios where we have to run after promised donation money on a monthly basis. Members can always donate more than $60.- but we can't offer them any additional rewards for doing so.

Privileges:

  • Unlock unique tag on TeamSpeak and the website.
  • Unlock Contributor signature on the forums.
  • Will be named in a "thank you" post on front page of the website.
  • Gain access to cosmetic facewear items in coop events:
    • Any type of half facial bandana / shemagh
    • Any type of full facial balaclava
    • Any type of bandana / glasses mix
  • Gain access to monthly NCO meetings (Gold only)

The Gold contributors gain access to the NCO meetings in order to observe what their funds are being spent and how decisions concerning the community are concluded. They are invited to be spectators during these meetings, however some topics will be marked by the meeting leader as "feedback sensitive". On these topics the NCOs will ask for opinions and ideas from present Contributors to get a feeling for the general mood of the community. Attendance to NCO meetings are not mandatory for Contributors, but we do require a response such as attending or "not attending" similar to regular events.

Interviewing

Prerequisite:

  • Minimum age 21+.
  • Has TeamViewer installed.
  • Has Arma3Sync troubleshooting knowledge.
  • Is online on TS outside of event times as much as possible.
  • Is mature and has good people skill.

Duties:

  • Approach people waiting in the Help Desk channel asap.
  • Interview applicants by following the Interview Handbook guidelines.
  • Test mods with the applicant after he has downloaded them.
  • Continue to help recruits with their questions as much as possible.
  • Help with any technical difficulties, e.g. installing the mods.

Public Relations

Prerequisite:

  • Is fluent in speaking and writing English.
  • Is polite and mature in internet media discussions.
  • is proficient in using internet media.

Duties:

  • Be a "war photographer", provide awesome screenshots.
  • Be a "war reporter", writing forum threads about operations.
  • Ensure we get a regular flow of votes on Armaclans page.
  • Maintain the BI forum thread. (21+ restriction applies)
  • Contact players looking for squad on BI forums. (21+ restriction applies)
  • Be active on Reddit Arma page. (21+ restriction applies)
  • Run Twitter account. (21+ restriction applies)

NOTE: It is possible to specialise in one or two things from the list above. Advise with Head of Public Relations for details.

Video Making

Prerequisite:

  • Has hardware capable of recording game play in 45+ FPS.

Duties:

  • Record videos in a way which is compliant with Video Making Handbook.
  • Edits videos in a way which is compliant with Video Making Handbook.
  • Publish videos in a way which is compliant with Video Making Hanbook.

NOTE: If you don't know how to edit videos, you can record them and upload them for others to edit. Advise with Head of VIdeo Making for details.
