Rules & Regulations

Failure to obey the following rules can result in penalties up to, and including, being discharged from the community.

General Rules

1
Minimum age for membership is 21+. Exceptions can be made for members between the age of 18-20 if they are evaluated mature enough during the interview - once accepted they will be on probation until one month after passing the Grunt Qualification Form (GQF).
2
Treat all members with respect, no matter what rank or age.
3
Discrimination or prejudice of any kind is unacceptable. Homophobia, sexism or racism will not be tolerated.
4

Reading Dslyecxi’s Full Tactical Guide to Arma 3 is a mandatory prerequisite for all members. As a Recruit if you want to advance to Grunt you will have to read the Grunt related ShackTac pages before filling out the Grunt Qualification Form (GQF).

  • Recruits: pages 8-92, 110-120
  • Grunts: pages 8-120, 152-206, 216-221
  • JrNCO's: pages 8-346

Note: this refers to the pages of the document, indicated in the white bottom bar - not the slides of the PDF view.

Event Regulations

28
Do not message NCO’s and officers who are already in game during an operation / training session. They are busy trying to get everyone organised so the event won’t be delayed and they won’t have time to respond to pokes or messages.
