Community Qualifications

Community qualifications are accomplished by attending and passing rigorous assessments. All members who gain qualifications gain priority in their respective roles but are not limited to those roles. Well done to all that have gained them. All qualifications expire after 4 months.

Helicopter Pilot Qualification: This is awarded to all members that have successfully passed the Helicopter Pilot Assessment.

Community Training Ribbons

Community Training Ribbons are awarded to community members that attend specialised trainings. All qualifications expire after 4 months.

Airborne Training Ribbon: This is awarded to members of the community that have successfully attended and passed Airborne Training.

Community Rewards

Community Rewards are given to members during our Campaigns and are earned for various reasons described below for each Reward.

Operation Red Storm Campaign Ribbon: Awarded to community members that served on 3/4 missions during the Campaign.
